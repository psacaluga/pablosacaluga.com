##### Context

During my time at Emergya, a digital consultancy specialized in Google Cloud, I worked on designing and optimizing AI-powered conversational experiences and user-centered digital products for leading brands across different industries.

##### Experience

As a Conversational Designer, I led the design of AI-driven virtual assistants for [Meliá Hotels International](../emergya-melia), [Liberbank](../emergya-liberbank), and [EVO Bank](../emergya-evo), improving customer interactions while optimizing operational efficiency. I collaborated with computational linguists, developers, and product managers to craft intuitive conversational flows, refine natural language processing (NLP) models, and ensure a seamless omnichannel experience.

As a UX Designer, I contributed to projects for INTEF, [Siemens](../emergya-sdt), and [Alter Technologies](../emergya-doeeet), focusing on usability, accessibility, and design consistency. My role involved conducting user research, defining interaction patterns, and developing interfaces that aligned with business objectives and user needs.
