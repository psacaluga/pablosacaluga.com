##### Context

Astara Connect is a platform for linking connected vehicles (hardware or OEM Cloud) with mobility services (EV chargers, parking, etc.). It facilitates the management and understanding of data for drivers, fleet managers, or companies in real time.

Where and when do you need to charge my EV? How was your fleet's behavior? How can you automate my fleet alerts in my system? Based on the ingested data, the product was designed to answer these questions and many more, where We were previously normalized and simplified for their consumption via API or the Platform.
