At The Big House, I worked remotely from Jerez as an UX and Interaction designer, teaming up with developers and other creatives on projects like [Spotahome](../tbh-spotahome), Lexvisors, Fontown, Material Fest (Cádiz) and thesingulars.

I was involved in end-to-end design, always making sure the experience was clean, consistent, and user-focused. I also helped shape and maintain design systems to keep things scalable and smooth across platforms.

#### Contributions

- Led the full design workflow, from discovery to final implementation, ensuring a user-centered approach aligned with business objectives.
- Redesigned and structured the design system to improve consistency, scalability, and efficiency across platforms.
- Collaborated closely with developers to implement UI components and enhance the overall user experience.
- Worked across multiple projects adapting design strategy to different business needs.
- Balanced UX/UI design with strategic thinking to deliver seamless and scalable product experiences.
- Organized and co-hosted design and development events to foster knowledge sharing and strengthen the community.
