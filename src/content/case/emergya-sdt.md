Siemens needed a more efficient and consistent way for engineers and designers to build and document complex system architectures. The challenge was to define a structured design system that improved usability while aligning with industry standards.

#### Responsabilities

- **Interaction design & usability**: Defined usability heuristics and interface principles tailored to Siemens’ engineering workflows.
- **Design system definition**: Structured interface design rules and visual patterns to ensure consistency across technical tools.
- **Documentation & enablement**: Delivered a full set of documentation—including a style guide, component library, and structural guidelines—for future development and onboarding.
