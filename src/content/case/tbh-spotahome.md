The main goal was to redesign Spotahome’s user experience to enhance trust and highlight the platform’s key differentiators, such as the presence of “runners” (on-site verifiers) and detailed neighborhood insights. This redesign aimed to better support users in their decision-making process when booking a property.

In addition to the core objective, the project also focused on:
- Defining a scalable design strategy for future growth.
- Crafting a tone and language tailored to different user profiles.
- Supporting content communication through a highly visual experience.

#### Contributions

- **Responsive design** across multiple breakpoints.
- Implementation of **Atomic Design** principles and a scalable **style guide**.
- **Internationalization (i18n)** support across languages.
- Facilitation of **UX Workshops** to align with business and user needs.
- In-depth **UX Research**: competitor analysis, ideation sessions, and user journey mapping.
- Development of **prototypes** for user testing and stakeholder review.
- Use of **SMACSS** and **BEM** methodologies for maintainable CSS architecture.
