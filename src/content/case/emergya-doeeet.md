DoEEEt by ALTER Technologies needed to simplify access to critical technical data on over 22 million Hi-Rel components for space applications. The challenge was to design an intuitive B2B platform that streamlined engineers’ decision-making by centralizing fragmented manufacturer information.

##### Responsabilities

- **UX & Interaction Design**: Led the design of the search, filtering, and comparison features.
- **User Research & Testing**: Conducted discovery workshops, usability testing, and data analysis to refine the experience.
- **Prototyping & Validation**: Created interactive prototypes to test and iterate on navigation flows and feature usability.
- **Design Documentation**: Produced UX specifications, component behaviors, and API requirements to guide development.
